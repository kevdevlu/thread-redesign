Leadership Introduction

Introductory is not the same as basic. In fact, as we set out on the journey of Thread, introduction is essential. The goal of this first set of educational modules is to help you orient yourself with yourself, your leadership, your ministry, and Thread. These teaching and preaching resources are novel. As with anything new, there will be a learning curve. As you become oriented, it is our hope that you will see the value that Thread has for the enrichment of you as a leader and your congregation. Every Thread series will have a Leadership Thread for the leader. Feel free to pass these on to anyone who will be speaking or leading during this series.

Our goal is not to give you handouts, but rather, to produce integrated leaders who are experiencing personal growth with help from these resources. Our hope is that the excitement of deeper learning and personal engagement will be passed on to your churches not just through Thread and its resources, but through you, as the Spirit works. More than anything, these resources for leaders are being created to help you come alive in Christ.

 Starting Point
For these first eight modules, you’ll start by doing the work of a prophet by following prompts and making honest assessments about yourself, your leadership, and your ministry. It’s hard to know where you are going if you don’t step back and take an honest look at where you are starting. Then, the lessons that follow will help you get in the flow of learning and leading with Thread’s resources. Our hope is that you will see impact, even before you preach your first Thread sermon, as you create space for and devote energy to the reflective lessons of this first module.
