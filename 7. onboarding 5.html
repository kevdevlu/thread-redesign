Module 1: Self Awareness

ALEPH -  Psalm 119:1-8 
Meditate: Walk in the way of the Lord

 Answer:  Take a moment to reflect on your journey into Christ, into leadership, and to where you are now. How has God been leading you? 

 BETH - Psalm 119:9-16
With my whole heart I seek you

How is your relationship with God going? Do you feel connected or is there distance? Why or why not? Is there anything keeping you from giving your whole heart to God?

 GIMEL - Psalm 119:17-24
Deal bountifully with your servant

How is your health? In what ways are you living a bountiful life or experiencing scarcity? Pray and take an inventory of bounty/health in each of these areas?

 DALETH - Psalm 119:25-32
My soul melts away for sorrow

Take a moment to reflect on any discouragement you might be experiencing? Share any defeats or disappointments that come to mind.
