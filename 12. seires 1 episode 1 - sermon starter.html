Series 1: Starting Point Introduction

Introduction
Sermon Starters

Focus (Big Idea):
 At the beginning of a new faith journey, we should examine if our ideas about God are limiting or expansive.

 Function (Desired Result):
 In Luke 15, Jesus challenges the Pharisees and scribes to examine their view of God by telling three stories that illustrate who God is and then ending with a challenge that encouraged them to expand their ideas about God. The image of God that we carry with us day to day can free us to live joyful lives in Christ, or it can weigh us down. At the starting point, it is essential for us to examine our image of God.
