Greetings everyone, This week in Thread, we focus our attention on the Law & the Prophets. The Torah (law) contains some 613 commandments (mitzvot), which cover everything from dietary restrictions to treating skin diseases to sexual ethics to the role and function of the priesthood. As contemporary followers of Jesus, how are we to understand the role of Torah in our lives? One of the primary questions we will consider this week is what the Torah teaches us about God and his disposition towards his people. How does the Torah reflect God's heart of loving-kindness that he desires to show us and instill in us? I hope and pray that the Spirit blesses you as you meditate on the Torah and that through it you experience the loving-kindness of our heavenly Father. May his teachings and instructions continue to create in us a heart of loving-kindness as well.

Grace & Peace

Thread Week #23 - The Presence

Greetings Friends, This week, our church family meditates on the theme of Presence. In the Thread podcast, Dave and Hannah are joined by renowned scholar Bernard McGinn for a conversation on Christian mysticism. Initially, the concept of mysticism may be intimidating or even off-putting for many of us. Dave, Hannah, and Bernard do an outstanding job of de-mystifying mysticism. Drawing on the biblical tradition and the examples and writings of men and women through Christian history, we are reminded that God's desire is not just that we know him intellectually but that we experience him relationally. May our hearts, minds, and souls be opened to experience God's presence and enjoy a continual relationship with him.

Grace & Peace
Jake

Greetings friends, This week, our journey through God's story takes us into the wilderness. This story of YHWH leading his people, Israel, out of slavery and into the wilderness, serves as a beautiful image for our own spiritual journeys. While a large part of us would love to live in victory on the far side of the sea, it is only through journeying in and through the wilderness that we can be shaped to maturity and know the joy available in the promised land. In the podcast, Dave & Hannah walk us through fundamental aspects of wilderness: 1. Our lives are interwoven with God. 2. Wilderness is meant to disorient & awaken. 3. Wilderness's purpose is to form & re-form. 4. We will always be in some state of wilderness. 5. God acts in love and for love in wilderness. May our time meditating on the biblical theme of wilderness help us locate ourselves on our own journey, encourage us, and deepen our trust in the God who lovingly leads us.
Grace & Peace, Jake
